SERVATHON
2021

Solve day-to-day social issues faced by people

About

Servathon Is A Hackathon Event That Serves As A Chance For Young Minds to solve the Social Issues Faced By People Of Different Sectors In Their Day-To-Day Life

Themes

Healthcare

  1. Maintain or improve the health via prevention, diagnosis, treatment, recovery and other physical and mental impairments in people.
  2. It includes Rural medical care, Gap between doctors and patients, Cold Chain Management

Governance

  1. Comprises all of the processes of governing. The system by which entities are directed and controlled. It is concerned with processes for decision making, accountability, and control.
  2. It includes increase in use of local products and services, Personal Safety/ Alerting system, Fighting Corruption.

Sustainable Environment

  1. Responsible interaction with the environment to avoid depletion or degradation of natural resources and allow for long-term environmental quality.
  2. It includes Energy Harvesting, Green Infrastructure, Waste Management.

Agriculture

  1. Agriculture is the backbone of the economy, but farmers do not benefit as much as it would be expected. Investigate the problems that affect farmers.
  2. It includes Crop Monitoring, Land Planning and Capital Investment Management, Detecting harmful additives / Preservatives.

Team Formation

Instructions

  1. A team can have a maximum of 5 members.
  2. Team can be intercollege or intra college and can combine with any department.
  3. Each team will be assigned a mentor to clarify their queries.
  4. The Same person cannot be in more than one team.

Timeline

Date Event Description
07/02/2021
Launch

The event servathon will be launched on February 7 and registration will start from February 7.

13/02/2021
Registration closes

Participants from any colleges and from different departments can apply for the hackathon as a team on the given 4 domain until the last date.

13/02/2021
Registration confirmation

Confirmation mail will be send to all registered participants. Along with the mail participants will be provided with guidelines and template (for problem statement submission) and a google form link for submitting problem statement.

14/02/2021
Introduction Session

There will be a session in the evening to provide instructions and to tell about the event flow.

17/02/2021
Problem statement submission

Registered teams have to select a problem statement in the selected domain and problem statement on or before the due date in the given template.

18/02/2021
Phase 1

All teams should explain their problem statement to the domain experts. Experts will evaluate their statement and shortlist the teams.

18/02/2021
Phase 1 result

The teams selected by the experts will be informed by mail. Those teams should start working on solution for their problem statement. Instructions for solution PPT and submission Google form link will be provided with mail.

20/02/2021
Solution of shortlisted teams

Teams should submit the solution of their problem statement. Solution should be submitted in PPT as per the given instructions.

21/02/2021
Final pitch

Teams should explain their solution idea to domain experts. Experts will decide the winners based on their evaluation.
